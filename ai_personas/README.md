# AI Persona Development

This project area is dedicated to the research, design, and development of various AI personas. These personas may be used for robot interaction, automated communication tasks (like conducting surveys), or other applications.

## Goals
- To explore how AI personas can enhance user experience with robots.
- To develop believable and engaging personalities for different roles.
- To investigate the technical implementation of these personas, likely leveraging Large Language Models (LLMs).

## Sub-Projects
- **[Robot Personas](./robot_personas/README.md):** Developing distinct personalities for robots like Joule and Perl.
- **[Survey Personas](./survey_personas/README.md):** Creating AI agents capable of conducting surveys or interviews.
